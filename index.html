About Me

I have completed an M.Tech in Computer Science at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata, where my academic and project work focused on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision. Previously, I earned an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University, which strengthened my analytical and quantitative foundations.

I have worked on diverse projects, including implementing Model Inversion Attacks and Membership Inference Attacks from scratch, developing algorithmic portfolio management strategies using Black-Litterman and Risk Parity models, and building applications such as a movie recommendation system, customer churn prediction models, and market basket analysis pipelines. I also explored autonomous control of the Lunar Lander environment using reinforcement learning and implemented dynamic memory allocation systems from scratch.

Beyond research, I worked as a Secondary School Mathematics Teacher, which helped me develop strong communication and mentoring skills. My technical expertise spans Python, C, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, SQL, and modern data science libraries.

My research interests lie in AI security, trustworthy machine learning, financial AI, and reinforcement learning, with the long-term goal of contributing to secure, interpretable, and privacy-preserving AI systems for real-world applications. My M.Tech dissertation, under the supervision of Professor Sarbani Palit, Head of the Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Unit (CVPRU), ISI Kolkata, focused on discovering a query-efficient Model Inversion Attack, contributing novel insights into the vulnerabilities and defenses of modern machine learning models.
